Cardiology Services

Our Cardiology Department offers comprehensive heart care services for the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of heart-related conditions. From routine checkups to advanced cardiac procedures, we are committed to providing exceptional care to ensure the well-being of your heart.

Cardiac Diagnostics

We offer a range of diagnostic tests including ECG, echocardiography, stress tests, and more to assess heart health and detect any potential issues.

Cardiac Treatments

Our team of specialists provides advanced treatment options for various heart conditions including coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and more.

Preventive Cardiology

We focus on preventing heart disease through lifestyle counseling, cholesterol management, blood pressure control, and screening for at-risk individuals.

Cardiac Surgery

Our hospital offers cutting-edge cardiac surgery for conditions such as heart bypass surgery, valve replacement, and minimally invasive procedures.
